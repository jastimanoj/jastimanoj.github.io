Tennessee Veterans Can Receive VA Disability Benefits For Many Injuries

There are lots of mental and physical impairments that Tennessee veterans can receive VA disability benefits compensation for. As a general rule, if your impairment is related to your military service it may be considered service-connected. Some of the most common impairments veterans are receiving money for is:

  • Agent Orange Exposure
  • Burn Pit Toxic Exposure
  • Dental Problems
  • Depression in Soldiers
  • Diabetes
  • Fibromyalgia
  • Gulf War Syndrome
  • Ischemic Heart Disease
  • Military Sexual Trauma (MST)
  • Posttraumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
  • Sleep Apnea
  • Traumatic Brain Injury (TBI)
